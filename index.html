KSEA at UC Berkeley

We are an association for scientists and engineers of Korean heritage at UC Berkeley.

Introduction to KSEA

KSEA was established in 1971 as a non-profit professional organization and has grown to over 6,000 registered members with over 70 local chapters and 13 technical groups, and 27 affiliated Professional Societies (APS) covering all major branches of science and engineering. KSEA aims to promote the application of science and technology for the general welfare of society, foster international cooperation especially between the U.S. and Korea, and help Korean-American Scientists and Engineers develop their full career potential.

Introduction to KSEA at UC Berkeley

We are a student organization at the heart of the UC Berkeley campus, as part of the Silicon Valley Chapter in KSEA. We hold biweekly general meetings for our members, organize workshops for research and career advancements, conduct exciting student-led projects in all facets of science and engineering, and hold socials.
